Since the studio was founded in 1985, Rare’s thirty‐five year video game pedigree has carried players worldwide on adventures to exotic lands, introducing them to all manner of brave heroes and vicious villains along the way. Whether it’s icons of the 90s like Banjo‐Kazooie and the Battletoads or more recent arrivals like the cheery Pirate Lord who welcomes you to Sea of Thieves, everyone who plays a Rare game will find their own favourite as they play.Now, thanks to our partnership with THG, players are able to salute their favourites in style with a broad range of products that celebrate Rare games old and new, hand‐picked by the team at Rare to ensure they’re fun and faithful to the games that inspired them. We’ll be looking to add new collections and expanding our ranges over time, so please check back regularly to see what’s been added. Happy shopping!
